#####  11.7 Standard vs Express Comparison

1.  Standard
   -  Price: 25$ per 1M transitions
   -  my StateMachine has 10 transitions
   -  1 M executions * 10 trans/exec = 10M transitions
   -  total 250$
2.  Express
   -  Price:
      -  1$ per 1M executions
      -  0.00001667$ per GB*s
   -  1M executions:
      -  1$ (requests)

   -  total 3.87$
